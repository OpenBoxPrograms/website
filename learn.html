What is OpenBox?

OpenBox Programs tackles resource and education inequity at its roots by presenting a free, public curriculum that builds core soft skills and high-level computational thinking in middle school students.
The program will also select a separate group of students from underprivileged backgrounds to receive direct guidance from mentors, culminating in the development of a high-level project. This sets students up for future, accelerated learning in CS and develops the skills needed to explore advanced topics independently, increasing representation in the computer science field.
