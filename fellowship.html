GLOBAL LEADERSHIP FELLOWSHIP.

The Global Leadership Fellowship ignites the next generation of leaders to forge community initiatives for social good. OpenBox Programs believes strongly in engaging future generations to foster an interest in technology and education. Through the fellowship, high school upperclassmen and undergraduate students will

  • Engage with younger generations from all backgrounds through direct community service,
  • Elaborate on community problems and propose solutions in a work experience setting, and
  • Expand on ideas with a final proposal.
You are invited to apply to our fellowship program. As a fellow, you will be guided through the process of proposing and creating a community initiative. This eight week program will guide applicants through the proposing and review of their ideas. Additionally, to develop communication skills and as an act of community service, fellows must log at least 15 hours of community service over the course of the eight week program. For example, select fellows may be invited to instruct programming languages to younger students, while others encouraged to host workshops.

Fellowship Eligibility:
To be eligible to apply, applicants must
  • Be in at least your junior year of high school or pursuing undergraduate studies at an accredited university in the United States,
  • Be fluent in English, and
  • Have consistent access to the Internet and a device (e.g. laptop, desktop computer).
Prior knowledge in Python or another high-level programming is recommended, but not required.
